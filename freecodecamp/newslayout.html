Heavy Rain Causes Flooding in Mumbai

Mumbai, Dec 28: Continuous rainfall for the last two days has caused severe flooding in several parts of Mumbai. Roads were waterlogged, and traffic movement was badly affected. Many schools remained closed for safety reasons. Rescue teams and municipal workers are working to clear water and help stranded people. Authorities have advised citizens to stay indoors unless necessary.

Bus Accident Leaves Ten Injured

Chandigarh, Dec 28: Ten people were injured when a passenger bus collided with a car near Sector 44 yesterday evening. The injured were immediately taken to a nearby hospital. Police have started investigating the cause of the accident. Eyewitnesses said the bus was overspeeding. Traffic remained affected for over an hour.
